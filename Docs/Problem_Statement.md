# Problem Statement

Diabetes is a major global health concern that affects millions of people. Early diagnosis plays an important role in reducing complications and improving patient outcomes.

Existing machine learning models mainly focus on prediction accuracy but often behave as black-box systems, making it difficult for healthcare professionals and patients to understand the prediction process. Additionally, most systems stop after predicting diabetes and do not provide actionable lifestyle recommendations.

The proposed project aims to develop an explainable machine learning framework that predicts diabetes risk while providing understandable explanations and personalized lifestyle recommendations.
